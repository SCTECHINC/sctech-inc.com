## 2. Positioning & Voice

### What SCTech Inc. is

A senior, hand-picked consulting team for companies that need a real builder in the room — not a staffing agency, not a 200-person firm with juniors on the keyboard.

We come in, understand the problem, design the right thing, and ship it. AI-native when it helps. Boring and reliable when that is what the work calls for.

### What we are not

- Not a body shop.
- Not a re-seller.
- Not a "we'll figure it out" group — every engagement is led by someone with 20+ years in.

### Voice & tone for the website

- Confident, plain English, no buzzword salad.
- Short sentences. Proof over adjectives — cite results (70%+ turnaround reduction, 25% under budget, 1,300+ craftsmen platform) instead of saying "world-class."
- Technical when the page calls for it; no apologies for being technical.
- Zero stock-photo vibes. Design reference is the Claude design file `SCTECH+INC.html` Spencer shared.
